How to Play Crosswordle?

01

Understanding the Grid

The game features a crossword-style grid where words intersect. Each word is five letters long, and letters at intersections must work for both across and down words.
Color Guide:
Not in word
Wrong position
Correct position
02

Making Your Guesses

Select a direction (across or down) and enter your five-letter word guess. The colors will show how close you are to both intersecting words.

Remember: Letters at intersections affect both words, so choose carefully!

03

Using the Feedback

Use the color feedback to deduce correct letters and positions. Pay special attention to intersection points as they provide clues for multiple words.

Green letters are locked in their positions. Yellow letters need new positions. Gray letters should be avoided.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often are puzzles updated?

A new puzzle is released daily at midnight. All players receive the same puzzle, allowing you to compare strategies and attempts with friends.

Is there a time limit?

No, take your time to solve the puzzle. However, you only get one puzzle per day, so make your guesses count!

How do intersections work?

Letters at intersections must work for both the across and down words. This means these positions need extra consideration as they affect multiple words simultaneously.

What's the best strategy for beginners?

Start with common five-letter words that have frequently used letters (like S, T, R, N, and vowels). Focus on intersection points first, as these positions affect multiple words and provide more clues. Practice with the tutorial mode to get familiar with the mechanics.

Can I play previous puzzles?

While the main focus is on daily puzzles, you can practice your skills in tutorial mode. Each day brings a fresh challenge, encouraging players to return daily for new puzzles and compare their solving strategies with friends.

How do I track my progress?

The game keeps track of your daily statistics, including win streaks, average attempts, and completion time. You can share your results with friends without revealing the answers, making it fun to compare strategies and achievements.

What happens if I get stuck?

If you're finding a puzzle particularly challenging, try taking a break and coming back with fresh eyes. Remember to use the color feedback from your previous guesses and focus on the intersection points. You can also practice similar patterns in tutorial mode to improve your skills.
